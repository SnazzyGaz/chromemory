How to play
A colour is shown for a few seconds. Memorise it. Then use the sliders to recreate it as accurately as you can. Five rounds per game — your score is averaged across all of them.
The three axes
H — Hue
The base colour — red, green, blue, etc. Measured in degrees around a colour wheel, 0–360.
S — Saturation
How vivid or washed out. 0% is grey, 100% is fully vivid.
B — Brightness
How light or dark. 0% is black, 100% is full brightness.
Scoring
Each axis is scored 0–100 based on how close your guess was. They're then combined: Hue counts for 50%, Saturation 30%, Brightness 20%.

Seeds
Every game has a seed — a short code that locks in the exact set of colours. Share it with a friend to challenge them on the same colours. Enter their seed on the home screen to play their game.
